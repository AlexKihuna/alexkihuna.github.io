    The Seasoned Woman

    Receive the revelation and confirm what you have always suspected...

    God created you to fulfill a specific purpose.

    A SEASONED WOMAN,
    is one who has learnt to embrace the varied seasons of life and to allow those seasons to sculpt and mould her into a vessel of strength, dignity and honor until she can confidently and unapologetically say, "WHEN I GROW UP, I WANNA BE ME!!!"

    A SEASONED WOMAN,
    is one who knows who she is and the purpose for which she was created and she knows that she is uniquely endowed with the gifting and skills to enable her fulfill that purpose.

    A SEASONED WOMAN,
    is one who knows the Mountain of Influence in the society and nation that she is called to influence and she knows that she is an answer and a solution to her society and nation and that she is among those that her society and nation have been waiting for.

    Become a seasoned woman today

    As a woman in the Market place, you are assigned and destined to influence and impact a particular Mountain in your society and nation.

    Here is a forum for you to engage, be provoked, challenged and encouraged to discover and own your Mountain and purpose and to also discover the power of seasons in equipping and empowering you to fulfill your purpose. Here you meet and learn from passionate women who have discovered and are already influencing their Mountains.

    Come and join me in learning how to be a voice in our society and nation as influencers and policy makers for a better nation.

    Sally Mahihu
